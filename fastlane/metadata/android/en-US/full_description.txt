<b style="color: dodgerblue;">ColorBlendr</b> is an application to modify Material You colors of your device.
Elevate your creativity with effortless material customization. Instantly tweak colors for a personalized touch in just a few taps.

<b>Features:</b>

- Accent saturation changer
- Background saturation changer
- Background lightness changer
- Pitch black theme in dark mode
- Manual color overriding
- and many more...

<b>Requirements:</b>

- Android 12+ device
- Working Root or Shizuku environment

<b>Instructions:</b>

- Install and open the app
- Allow permissions for the app
- Select root or shizuku mode
- That's it. Now you are good to go!

<b>Translation:</b>

Assist in translating ColorBlendr into your preferred language through <a href="https://crowdin.com/project/ColorBlendr">our Crowdin platform</a>. Your contribution will help make ColorBlendr accessible to a wider audience.

<b>Alarming permissions:</b>

- <u>QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES</u>: Required for individually theming 3rd party apps which has monet theming support but still does not follow system colors.
- <u>MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE</u>: Required for grabbing the current wallpaper and extract dominant colors from the wallpaper.
